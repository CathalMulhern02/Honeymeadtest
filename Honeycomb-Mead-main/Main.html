What is Mead?

Mead (Miodh in Irish) is the world's oldest alcoholic brew, the primary ingredient of this ancient drink is honey however this does not mean that all mead is as sweet as honey since it can be incredibly diverse - ranging from dry to sweet, and pairing well with combinations of other ingredients, such as fruit or spices. Our award-winning mead is judged as among the best in the world, packed full of delicious flavours and embodying the modern, refined style of mead making. Our mead is made from pure raw honey and carefully picked berries, carefully crafted using our very own authentic recipes.



Mead, and it's fruit-flavoured counterpart; Melomel are both wine-like brews, most being made in the style of such, however this does not restrict it from other varieties.



Mead comes in many different syles, flavours and methods, we are certain that you will find one you enjoy.



All of our mead is made using Irish honey, freshly picked fruit and clear, spring water, to ensure the highest of standards.

Our Story

Our origins begin with all three of our founders' strong interest in three fields. Brewing, marketing and web development. These three interests combined led us to form our company; Honeycomb Mead.

The initial scale of the work was limited to our own home-brew production, but thankfully, due to our initial success, our facilities have now expanded to a larger, more sophisticated brewery.

For us, the passion we share for creating and selling a product we truly care about is what drives us to excel. We hope that with the continued support of our wonderful customers, we may go on to improve our skills, business and products.
